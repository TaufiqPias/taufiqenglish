Online Examination - Open for All

Please be honest while answering the questions. Do not seek online help. Use your own knowledge.

By: Taufiq

Instruction

Every Friday before 11:59 PM, a new online exam will be available. You can take the exam anytime. Once you start, you have 60 minutes to complete it. Each exam consists of 100 multiple-choice questions. After submission, your score will be displayed immediately. You can retake the exam as many times as you like to improve your score. Good luck!

Rules

  • 60 minutes - 100 questions per exam.
  • 1 mark for each correct answer. 0.25 mark will be deducted for each incorrect answer.
  • No negative marking for unattempted questions. If not sure, you can SKIP any question.
  • Once you select an answer, you cannot change it. So be careful.
  • You can retake the exam multiple times.
  • Your highest score will be recorded.
  • Do not refresh the page during the exam. Your progress will restart.
